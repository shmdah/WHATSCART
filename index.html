Frequently Asked Questions

Do I get a website link?

Yes! You get a free custom link in the format: businessname.whats-cart.com. You can share this on Instagram, TikTok, and WhatsApp.

Can I use my personal number?

We highly recommend using a dedicated WhatsApp Business number. This keeps your personal chats separate from customer orders and looks more professional.

Can I update my products?

Yes. To update products, change prices, or remove items, simply contact our customer team on WhatsApp to raise a ticket. We handle all technical updates for you to keep things hassle-free.

How do payments work?

Payments are handled directly between you and the customer on WhatsApp. We do not hold your money. You can request M-Pesa, Cash, or Bank Transfer directly in the chat.
